School Catalogue App - Catalogue

Description:


Catalogue is an online school catalogue useful for students and teachers, to keep track of their grades and overall educational situationship. It can be used by schools, highschools or universities.

The project is a web-based application made using HTML, CSS, JavaScript for the front-end and Python(Flask) and SQLite3 for the back-end.

- Fully functional register and login -the ability to see all the grades of the student who is logged in (course, grade, date and time of the assignation
- Filter and sort for the grades based on course, grade
- The ability to add new grades
- The possibility to view advanced statistics such as:
- Top x students (x being a value given by the user)
- My rank in the system(based on the general average grade of all students registered)
- My general average for each course and the total general average
